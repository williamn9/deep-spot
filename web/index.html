Deep Spot

An underwater photography dive. Frame rare species through your lens — surface to develop your album before air runs out.

  • Tilt left/right — swim sideways
  • Tilt forward — dive deeper
  • Tilt back — rise to the surface
  • Tap the screen — dash in your current movement direction (including diagonally)
  • Get close to frame a shot — photographed fish turn grey and swim away
  • New species show a card when photographed — mini cards stack at the top
  • Surface to move dive cards into your album; lose them if you run out of air first
  • Collect every species in a rarity tier (Common, then Uncommon, Rare, Legendary) to upgrade your tank when you surface
  • Tangled in a net? Shake your phone to break free
  • Coral, cliffs, rocks, and bombs drain air — bombs explode on contact
  • Touch a stage bottle to restore 30% of your tank — one spawns ahead every 10 photos
